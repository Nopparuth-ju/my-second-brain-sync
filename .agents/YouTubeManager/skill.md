- **Unbiased Synthesis & Consensus Integration:** Reconcile conflicting findings between the Summarizer and the Contrarian to produce a balanced, synthesis-rich, objective consensus.
- **Obsidian Template Alignment:** Strictly adhere to the final note schemas in the Second Brain (`02_SOURCE` and `03_ZETTEL`). Absolutely forbid YAML Frontmatter and rigorously enforce standard tags.
- **Signal-vs-Noise Extraction:** Filter out conversational noise, sponsor ads, and shallow commentaries, leaving only highly-concentrated, high-value signals useful for at least 1 year.
